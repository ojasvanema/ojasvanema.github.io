This project focuses on predicting the probability of survival for colon cancer patients over a specified time duration.

**Key Achievements:**
*   Successfully used ML models such as **Cox Proportional Hazards Regression** and **Random Survival Forest**.
*   Achieved best accuracy using the Cox Proportional Hazard Regression model.
